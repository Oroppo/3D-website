My Name Is Dante Arruda

Welcome!

I love Games!

I adore the idea of building up a world seperate from our own; fit with it's own rules and history, it's an amazing feeling to be a part of something so special. I think that games give people the unique oppurtunity to connect with others in ways that just aren't otherwise possible and I want to facilitate those connections as much as possible.

Primary Skills:

I do most of my work in C++ and C#. I specialise in Graphics Programming and Sound Design. I've created Graphics Engines and systems using OpenGL to promote portability. I work in FL Studio to create my music and implement them using the FMOD API and it's Event Systems. I also have some minor experience in Design and 3D Modelling/Animations.
